About US

Working Together To Make World Better

Familyhood & Bright Life is a community-driven organization committed to strengthening the resilience and opportunities of vulnerable households in Kenya. We work to ensure that no child, youth, or caregiver is left behind.

We operate in Nairobi, Turkana, Kajiado, Machakos, and Nakuru, delivering integrated programs that expand access to education, improve sustainable livelihoods, enhance protection systems, and build community resilience.

Our Work

Our six-pillar strategy work

Familyhood & Bright Life delivers its work through six strategic pillars responding to the core issues affecting vulnerable households in our counties of operation.

Education, Mentorship & Learning Support

We ensure vulnerable children and youth have access to quality education and opportunities for growth.

Livelihoods & Economic Empowerment

We strengthen household income and resilience through empowerment programs and youth support initiatives.

Child Protection & Caregiver Support

We safeguard vulnerable children and strengthen family systems.

Health, WASH & Community Resilience

We promote healthier and safer communities.

Peacebuilding, Human Rights & Social Cohesion

We strengthen peaceful coexistence and civic participation.

Environmental Conservation & Climate Adaptation

We support environmentally responsible and climate-resilient communities.
